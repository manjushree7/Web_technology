He may not be known world wide and his invention may not be huge but he has contributed his life by bringing better and small important changes . He developed IBM's managment style and corporate culture from John Henry Patterson's traning at NCR .He turned the company into highly effective selling organization,based largly on punched card tabulating machines.A leading self made industrialist,he was one of the richest man of his time and was called world's greatest salesmen when he died in 1956 AD.
